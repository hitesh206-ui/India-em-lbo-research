# Assumptions Log

This file records modeling, data, and paper assumptions so that the working paper remains transparent and easy to audit.

## Deal Dataset Assumptions

| Assumption | Rationale | Status | Evidence Needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| India buyouts often use lower debt/EBITDA than US buyouts | Local acquisition-finance constraints and higher borrowing costs | Working assumption | Deal-level evidence and benchmark comparison |
| Hybrid instruments may substitute for classic acquisition debt | NCDs, CCDs, structured credit, and private credit may be more practical in India | Working assumption | Transaction documents, news, lender disclosures |
| Public data may underreport leverage | Indian transaction details are often not fully disclosed | Working assumption | Cross-check from multiple public sources |

## LBO Model Assumptions

| Assumption Area | Default Approach | Sensitivity Required |
|---|---|---|
| Entry multiple | EV/EBITDA based on comparable transactions | Yes |
| Debt quantum | Lower than US-style LBO base case | Yes |
| Interest cost | INR borrowing-cost assumption, not USD leveraged-loan assumption | Yes |
| Exit route | IPO, strategic sale, secondary sale, or pending | Yes |
| Holding period | 5 years as base case | Yes |
| Revenue growth | India/emerging-market growth case should be explicit | Yes |

## Regulatory Assumptions

Regulatory assumptions must be tied to a specific source, date, jurisdiction, and interpretation. The paper should avoid making legal conclusions without clearly labeling them as research assumptions.
