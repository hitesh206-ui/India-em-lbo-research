# Evidence Addendum - Version 3.0

This addendum records the public-source evidence added to the SSRN working-paper draft to reduce the risk that the manuscript is perceived as a generic framework.

## Purpose

The prior manuscript was structurally suitable for SSRN but remained heavily conceptual. Version 3.0 adds market evidence, transaction examples, regulatory anchors, and a source-quality classification to strengthen credibility.

## Evidence Added

| Evidence item | Source anchor | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| India PE-VC market scale | Bain & Company and IVCA, India Private Equity Report 2025 | Shows that India is large enough to justify buyout-market research. |
| Buyout relevance | Bain & Company and IVCA reported buyouts at 51% of PE deal value, up from 37% in 2022 | Supports the claim that control-oriented private equity activity is material. |
| Private credit depth | EY India Private Credit in India: H2 2025 update | Supports the role of private credit in India-adapted capital structures. |
| Acquisition financing use case | EY India reported refinancing, acquisition financing, and capex as meaningful use cases | Supports the use of private credit in acquisition-related financing. |
| Financial assistance constraint | Companies Act, 2013, Section 67 | Supports the claim that debt pushdown and target support cannot be assumed mechanically. |
| Takeover regulation | SEBI Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers Regulations, 2011, as amended | Supports the listed-company acquisition and open-offer discussion. |
| External borrowing framework | RBI Master Direction on ECB, Trade Credits and Structured Obligations | Supports the offshore-leverage and currency-constraint discussion. |
| Insolvency framework | IBC and IBBI corporate insolvency regulations | Supports creditor-enforcement and downside-credit-risk discussion. |
